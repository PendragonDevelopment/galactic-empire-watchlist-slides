Step 2: Encrypt business communications (PGP and GMail)

The fast and easy way: Mailvelope

https://www.mailvelope.com/

Mailvelope is a browser plugin that uses PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) to encrypt emails in popular webmail clients.

You can post your key on pgp.mit.edu so others can find your public key and send you encrypted messages.
